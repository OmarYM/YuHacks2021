Here are some tips you can use to stay safe at school!

Staying home when you are not feeling great helps you and others stay safe. Always try to keep others in mind befoer you step out the door.

Staying 6 feet apart helps you and others stay safe. Always try to practice social distancing. This helps prevent the spread of Covid-19 a step further.

Covid-19 is a deadly virus. This virus gets into your system from you touching your face or air borne particles. Keeping your mask on at all times ensures that you wont be touching your face on aciident or breathing in the virus directly.

Try your best to be very concious of where you place your hands. Keeping your mask on is great but sometimes we still touch the sides of our face or we touch our face as we take off our mask. Make sure you know how to take off your mask properly.

Make sure to wash your hands with soap for at least 20 seconds. This kills an immense amount of germs and helps keep you healthy. If you cant wash your hands use hand sanitizer as an alternative.

If you breathe in the same air as another person in a small space for too long this could put you at risk of getting the virus. If you are stationary in an area make sure its well ventilated to ensure your breathing clean air.

Fresh air clears your lungs. It has a great amount of oxygen and its safest place to take off your mask when you've had it on for a while. Just make sure your alone and enjoy a great fresh of air.
