Here are some tips you can use to stay safe at school!

1. Stay home if you arent feeling well.

2. When on campus try your best to maintain 6 feet apart.

3. Keep your face mask on at all times.

4. Avoid touching your face as much as possible.

5. Wash or sanitize your hands as frequency as possible.

6. Don't be in small areas for too long.

7. Make sure to always get some fresh air whenever you need it.
